Introducing Gight...

Gight is a automised safety light combining motion sensor and LED to guide you by night.
The motion sensor detects movement and lights up your customised night route (e.g. from bed to bathroom), highlighting hazardous points.

The Gight light saves :

- stretching to the light switch
- falling at night
- disturbing others sleeping

Our Mission

Gight is the result of a dedicated Student Company at Radboud University, Nijmegen. As a Social Company, our mission is to deliver customer safety to the elderly.

Gight is a Not-For-Profit Company. 100% of our income will be reinvested back into Gight and our customers. In purchasing a Gight light, you're not only investing in your own safety but also the safety of others.

We are dedicated to the communities we work in and to being socially and environmentally responsible. Gight's molecular design allows for an efficient recycling and repair service that minimises e-waste.

Service and Returns

Gight offers a home installation service for disabled and elderly customers who require assistance setting up their new night light. The cost of this service is dependent on your location, as our travel costs have to be considered. Please contact us for a quote if you require this service.

Should your Gight light stop working, we will repair/replace your product for free with as little disruption to you as possible. Where possible we will come to you to repair the device, otherwise we will send a prepaid postage bag for you to send us your product. The molecular design of Gight products allows for fast repairs, much recycling and little e-waste.
